## **SCENARIO:**

A premium Organic Food delivery service wants a modern,
"fresh" look. They need a brand identity that communicates
trust, health, and speed through colors and fonts.

## **MISSION:**

- Transform the wireframes into a High-Fidelity UI Design.

- Create a Style Tile: Choose a professional Color Palette and Typography.

- Design 3 Key Screens: Splash Screen, Product List, and Product Details.

- Focus on spacing, alignment, and high-quality imagery.


## **TOOLS:** 

Figma / Adobe XD, etc.
